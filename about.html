About Us

Minimal News & Current Affairs:
Your Source for Unbiased Insights

Minimal News & Current Affairs was founded in [Month Year], originating from a collective desire to provide clear, concise, and unbiased news coverage. We operate as a dedicated independent news platform, committed to expanding access to accurate information and fostering critical discourse among readers worldwide.

Under the slogan "Unveiling Truths, Shaping Perspectives," our platform is dedicated to delivering high-quality journalism that informs, educates, and engages. We aim to be your trusted source for understanding the complexities of current events.

Our Mission

The following are the four core principles we at Minimal News & Current Affairs wish to espouse:

  • I. Deliver Unbiased Reporting: We are committed to presenting facts objectively, allowing our readers to form their own informed opinions.
  • II. Foster Critical Thinking: Our content aims to encourage deep analysis and understanding of complex global and local issues.
  • III. Provide Diverse Perspectives: We seek out and highlight a range of viewpoints to ensure comprehensive coverage.
  • IV. Promote Digital Accessibility: We strive to make our news and analysis easily accessible through a user-friendly online platform.

History

  • July 2024: Minimal News & Current Affairs was conceptualized by a group of independent journalists.
  • August 2024: The core team was officially formed and initial planning began.
  • September 2024: The platform name "Minimal News & Current Affairs" was adopted.
  • October 2024: Website development commenced, focusing on a sleek and intuitive user experience.
  • January 2025: Beta testing and content curation phase initiated.
  • May 2025: The website was officially launched to the public.
